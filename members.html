YoonKi Kim

Principal Investigator

YoonKi Kim is a researcher in the Department of Mathematics at Yonsei University. His research focuses on black hole dynamics, quantum field theory, and analytic number theory, with notable contributions to the Kerr-to-Schwarzschild transition, Yang-Mills mass gap problem, and the Riemann Hypothesis.
